## Overview

BossHookah.site is a Shopify ecommerce system engineered for revenue velocity. The architecture prioritizes fast product discovery, bundle positioning, and checkout optimization to increase average order value and drive repeat purchases.

## System Architecture

Collection segmentation routes visitors to the right products quickly. Bundle positioning surfaces high-margin combinations at the product level. Upsell logic is embedded in the cart and checkout flow. Email capture is integrated into the purchase journey for lifecycle automation.

## Stack

Shopify, Conversion Optimization, Collection Architecture, Email Capture, Checkout Engineering

## Flow

Landing → Collection segmentation → Product focus → Upsell logic → Optimized checkout

## Results

Structured for measurable improvement in average order value and repeat purchase rate through systematic bundle positioning and checkout engineering rather than design aesthetics.
