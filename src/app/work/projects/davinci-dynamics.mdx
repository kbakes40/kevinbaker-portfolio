## Overview

DaVinciDynamics.ai is the operator-facing recruitment and onboarding infrastructure for the dealer network. The system is engineered to attract serious operators, filter out unqualified applicants early, and automate the onboarding sequence so no manual follow-up is required.

## System Architecture

Traffic enters through operator-positioned landing architecture that filters intent at the copy level. Qualified visitors complete a structured intake form. The backend routes submissions by qualification score, triggers email sequences, and initiates onboarding workflows automatically.

## Stack

Landing Architecture, Intake Logic, CRM Pipelines, Email Routing, Automation

## Flow

Traffic → Operator positioning → Qualification intake → Automated routing → Onboarding sequence

## Results

Reduced manual onboarding time and improved dealer quality by front-loading qualification logic into the funnel itself rather than the sales process.
